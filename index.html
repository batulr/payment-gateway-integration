    Our Mission

    HelpChild is a social campaign started by students to help the children from the under-priveleged get proper food, education, clothing and all other basic supplies. Giving hope, gives a pleasure and peace!

    Provide Education

    By coming together and gathering many young people who are students and would have a passion towards helping the society, the children can be thought basics thoroughly.

    Provide Healthy Food

    To achieve this goal we order food from budding caterers such that they get experience and help as well as the children can become nourished.

    Fund Raisers

    To raise funds for getting food and basic supplies for the homies, we arrange for campaigns or conduct events wherein people donate money.
